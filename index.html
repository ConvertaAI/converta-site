Common Questions

Everything you need to know.

Why is there a setup fee?
+
The setup fee covers real work: a strategy call, writing your AI's script, programming your FAQs, configuring phone routing, test calls, and QA before launch. Clients who invest in setup see ROI significantly faster.
Will callers know they're talking to AI?
+
Aria uses a natural, warm voice and conversational language. Most callers don't realize it's AI. If directly asked, Aria is transparent. Callers care far more about getting answers quickly than who answers them.
How long does setup take?
+
Most clients are live within 3 business days of the onboarding call. We handle everything — script writing, number setup, FAQ programming, and testing. You review and approve.
Can I keep my existing phone number?
+
Yes. We either port your existing number or set up call forwarding so your current number routes to Aria. Callers always dial the same number they always have.
Is there a long-term contract?
+
No contracts. Month-to-month after setup. Cancel with 30 days' notice at any time. Annual plans are non-refundable after 30 days. Setup fees are non-refundable since the work is done upfront.
